The Framework

Six Pillars of Passive Income

Each strategy has a different risk profile, timeline, and tax treatment. Understanding all six is how you build something that lasts.

Dividend Stocks

The backbone of most early retirement portfolios. Dividend growth stocks compound quietly for years before you feel the income. We cover yield vs. growth tradeoffs, payout ratios, and how to build a portfolio that raises your income every year.

REITs

Real estate exposure without a down payment or a tenant calling at midnight. REITs must distribute 90% of income, which makes them unusually reliable cash flow instruments - once you understand how to evaluate them beyond just yield.

Index Funds

The silent wealth builder. Total market index funds outperform the majority of active managers over 20-year periods. The real skill is doing nothing during the downturns - which is harder than it sounds and worth writing about honestly.

Rental Income

The most labor-intensive passive income on this list - and the one with the best tax treatment. We cover the honest math on cash-on-cash returns, vacancy rates, and why most landlords underestimate their true costs in year one.

Digital Products

The highest-margin income stream once established. Courses, templates, and financial guides have zero inventory cost. The catch: they require significant upfront effort and a distribution channel that most people underestimate.

Tax Strategy

The difference between a 3% and a 4.5% effective yield on a dividend portfolio is often just tax placement. Asset location, qualified dividends, depreciation recapture on rentals - the tax layer is where most passive income guides go silent.
