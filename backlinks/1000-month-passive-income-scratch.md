**Answer this question:** How much money do you need to generate $1,000 per month in passive income?

When I retired at 41, I didn't have $300,000 sitting around waiting to be deployed. I built it in phases over years-and each phase generated meaningful passive income that kept me motivated.

Here's what matters: $50,000 in dividend-yielding investments generates about $166/month. $100,000 generates roughly $333/month. You're not aiming for $300,000 on day one. You're aiming for your next milestone.

There's also flexibility in how you get there. Dividend stocks, bonds, rental income, digital products-each has different capital requirements and yield profiles. The person with $50,000 in bonds earning 4% might need $150,000 in dividend growth stocks earning 2% initially, but with appreciation potential.
